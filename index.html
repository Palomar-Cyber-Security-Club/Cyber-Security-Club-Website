Our Mission

The Palomar Cyber Security Club is a student-led organization dedicated to building real-world cybersecurity skills in a collaborative, hands-on environment. We believe security knowledge should be accessible to everyone — whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced practitioner.

From vulnerability scanning and penetration testing to networking labs and CTF competitions, we train the next generation of security professionals right here at Palomar College.
